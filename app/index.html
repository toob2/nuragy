About Us

We are a health care provider specialising in high end Health Care Assistants and Registered Nurses to a wide range of health institutions. We use our knowledge to recruit only the best and most suitable candidates. Our clients trust us to place the right staff in the right roles with many requests coming at short notice.

We have been dedicated to recruiting top quality healthcare workers since 1929.
